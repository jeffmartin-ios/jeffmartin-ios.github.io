Frequently Asked Questions

What audio formats does OS-39 support?

OS-39 supports FLAC, ALAC (Apple Lossless), and MP3. Files are played directly from the Files app with no importing step needed.

Do I need a subscription?

No. OS-39 is free with core playback features, and a single one-time Pro purchase unlocks gapless playback, the full 10-band EQ, all themes, exclusive visualizers, and enhanced recommendations.

Is my music library private?

Completely. OS-39 operates 100% offline with no data collection. Your library, listening habits, and preferences never leave your device.
