Frequently Asked Questions

What crafts can I track?

Pattern Projects is versatile! It's designed for sewing and knitting, but works great for embroidery, quilting, and other crafts.

Is my data private?

Yes. Your project data and photos are stored locally on your device and synced securely via your private iCloud account.

Can I track my stash?

Yes, the app includes a dedicated Stash section to help you organize your fabrics, yarns, and notions.
