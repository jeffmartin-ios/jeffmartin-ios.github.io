## What's New

Thanks to everyone who wrote in with suggestions — this update addresses the most common requests.

### Faster Gear Search

Searching your catalog is now significantly faster, particularly for larger collections. Results appear as you type with improved accuracy.

### Kit Sorting Options

You can now sort your kits by name, total value, or total weight. The preference is saved automatically between sessions.

### UI Refinements

- Gear detail screen layout improved for smaller iPhone models
- Purchase date input redesigned for faster entry
- Minor typography improvements throughout

## Bug Fixes

- Fixed an issue where kit weight wasn't updating immediately after editing a gear item
- Resolved a rare crash when deleting an item shared across multiple kits
- Corrected currency display inconsistency in the Collection Insights screen
