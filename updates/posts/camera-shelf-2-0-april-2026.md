Version 2.0 is officially here, and it brings a massive overhaul to the UI along with countless enhancements and new functionalities you’ve been asking for! 

Check out the full rundown of what’s new below. *As a special bonus for a limited time, you can also enjoy a lifetime unlock of Camera Shelf Pro at 50% off!*

### 🎨 A Complete UI Overhaul & General Polish
We've significantly upgraded the visual experience and functionality across the board:
- **New Grid Views:** You're no longer limited to the standard List view! You can now toggle a beautiful new Grid view and customize it between 2-column or 3-column layouts in your settings.
- **Streamlined Workflow:** We polished the Item and Kit screens, moving all of your most-used actions into a unified, central action bar. 
- **Smarter Finances:** Managing a global collection? Camera Shelf now intelligently converts and sums totals for items bought in multiple different currencies while cleanly retaining the original purchasing currency in your breakdowns.
- **Tactile Feel & Polish:** We’ve layered haptics into various buttons to bring the app to life right in your hands, along with a myriad of under-the-hood quality of life enhancements.

### 🤖 New Widgets & Siri Integration
Your gear is now accessible right from your iOS Home Screen!
- **Home Screen Widgets:** Add beautiful widgets for Quick Add, Recent Gear, Collection Value, and your Kit Packing Lists.
- **Siri Support:** Need answers hands-free? You can now explicitly ask Siri for details about your collection! (Check the app for specific prompts).
- **Shortcuts Automation:** Supercharge your workflow using Apple Shortcuts, including the ability to automate checking off items in a kit.

### 📸 Smarter Items & Kits
- **Battery Tracking:** We've added a highly requested "Batteries" category! You can now specifically track battery health and charge cycles for all your power packs.
- **Speedy Data Entry:** You can now duplicate items for incredibly fast cataloging of duplicate gear. We also expanded this to Kits, allowing you to favorite, duplicate, and filter them with a freshly updated UI.

### 🌗 Themes Your Way
We’ve fully enhanced our theme engine. You can now choose specifically between Dark Mode (default), Light Mode, or System (which automatically follows your iPhone's current theme).

**Enjoy the update!** If you love the changes, a glowing review on the App Store goes a very long way for our indie studio.
